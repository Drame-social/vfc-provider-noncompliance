# Limitations

1. All data are synthetic and generated for portfolio demonstration only.
2. The dataset does not contain real CDC, PEAR, provider, site-visit, patient, or jurisdiction performance data.
3. Synthetic rates were intentionally scaled and perturbed to avoid reproducing non-public aggregate outputs.
4. Causal inference is not appropriate; analyses are descriptive and program-monitoring oriented.
5. Domain compliance variables are simplified roll-ups and do not reproduce the full official site-visit instruments.
6. Provider categories, regions, and visit methods are realistic but synthetic.
7. Missingness is simulated and may not reflect any real-world missingness mechanism.
8. Dashboard findings are for workflow demonstration and should not guide real program operations.
